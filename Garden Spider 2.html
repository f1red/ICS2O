First time setup

Added Peripherals

The peripheraels will have to be connected to the computer. It is all wired. The monitor will be connect via an HDMI to the dedicated graphics card. The mouse, headphone, and keyboard will be connected to the computer (preferably the back) via USB. The drivers should be downloaded automatically upon connecting it to the computer. The computer will have wired internet connecting his router to the back of his computer. However, a wireless adapter can be added if wanted.
