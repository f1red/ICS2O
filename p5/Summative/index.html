Requirements for video game

Instructions:

***It is strongly recommended to close all other applications and tabs to ensure optimal preformance***(for toasters)
The Goal: Score the pucks into the red net at the top centre. Each hit in the net counts as 1 (one) point. You have two pucks, if both of your pucks fall out at the bottom of the arena, the game is over. The goal is to get the highest amount of points. Good luck!
